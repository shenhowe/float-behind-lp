floatbehind.ninja is automatic update of chat tips on your desktop.
Get tips that team members want to share and pin them on your desktop.

  1. Let's get started with floatbehind.ninja.

    Please download floatbehind.ninja (for Mac) and login with your Slack account. floatbehind.ninja collects shared information like technical tips from your team.

  2. Decide a command for the floatbehind.ninja.

    Please decide a command on Slack integration setting for floatbehind.ninja. Go to Slack integration setting page and set your prefered command name.

  3. Share your tips with the command.

    floatbehind.ninja will work on every Slack channel. Post URLs that you want to share with team members by using the command. floatbehind.ninja will pin it on your team member's desktop.
